What's important to me

To me, design is the process of transforming problems into meaningful solutions and ideas into tangible experiences. It’s driven by curiosity, uses research, empathy, and creativity. My goal is to create designs with people in mind and more.

User-centered design

Designing with the end-user in mind is the key of the process. Every decision is focused on creating user friendly and visually appealing designs that provide user the best experiences, ensuring their voice is reflected in every project.

Team culture

Working in a team is one of the most important aspect with either UX or developer role. Whether working with other developers, marketers, or business teams, collaboration drives success in each design projects.
